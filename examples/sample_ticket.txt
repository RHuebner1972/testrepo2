Title: Payment processing fails for international cards

Description:
Multiple users have reported that payment processing fails when using 
international credit cards (non-US issued cards). The error message 
displayed is "Payment failed. Please try again."

This issue has been reported by 15 users in the past week, primarily 
from European customers. US-based cards work fine.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Create an account or log in
2. Add items to cart
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Enter international credit card details
5. Click "Process Payment"
6. Error: "Payment failed. Please try again."

Expected Behavior:
Payment should process successfully for valid international cards.

Actual Behavior:
Payment fails with generic error message.

Technical Details:
- Payment provider: Stripe
- Error in logs: "currency_mismatch" 
- Affected regions: EU, UK, Australia, Canada

Business Impact:
- Lost revenue estimated at $5,000/week
- Customer complaints increasing
- Risk of losing international customers

Priority Assessment:
- Severity: High (revenue impact)
- Urgency: High (ongoing customer impact)

Workaround:
Customers can use PayPal as an alternative payment method.
