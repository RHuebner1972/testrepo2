Feature: Multi-Currency Support for International Payments

Background:
Our e-commerce platform needs to support international customers better.
Currently, we only support USD transactions, causing issues for international
customers and leading to cart abandonment.

Business Objectives:
- Increase international sales by 30%
- Reduce cart abandonment for international customers
- Improve customer satisfaction ratings

Stakeholders:
- Product Owner: Jane Smith
- Engineering Lead: John Doe
- Finance Team: Sarah Johnson

Requirements:

Functional Requirements:
1. Support multiple currencies (EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY at minimum)
2. Display prices in user's local currency based on their location
3. Allow users to manually select their preferred currency
4. Show currency conversion rates at checkout
5. Process payments in the customer's selected currency
6. Send receipts with both local currency and USD equivalent
7. Generate financial reports with multi-currency breakdown
8. Support refunds in the original transaction currency

Non-Functional Requirements:
1. Currency conversion rates should update at least every 4 hours
2. Checkout page load time must remain under 3 seconds
3. Support for 1000 concurrent international transactions
4. 99.9% uptime for payment processing
5. PCI DSS compliance must be maintained
6. GDPR compliance for EU customers

Technical Constraints:
- Must integrate with existing Stripe payment infrastructure
- Use exchange rates from a reliable API (OpenExchangeRates)
- Database schema changes must be backward compatible
- Must support existing mobile app versions

Acceptance Criteria:
- Users can complete purchases in any supported currency
- Exchange rates are accurate within 1% of market rate
- All existing USD functionality continues to work
- Financial reconciliation is possible across currencies

Out of Scope:
- Cryptocurrency payments
- Currency speculation features
- Historical exchange rate analysis

Timeline:
- Design: 2 weeks
- Development: 6 weeks
- Testing: 2 weeks
- Deployment: 1 week
- Target Launch: Q1 2026
